Coastal Investment Advisors, Inc. (Wilmington)

July 2024 · 4 minute read

Coastal Investment Advisors, Inc. is a large advisory firm based in Wilmington. It manages $394.34 million of regulatory assets for 2,698 client accounts. It has been registered with the SEC as an adviser since 2015 and has operated in the jurisdictions of Alabama, Alaska, Arizona, and 34 other states.

Coastal Investment Advisors provides financial planning services, portfolio management for individuals and small businesses, portfolio management for businesses and institutional clients, pension consulting services, selection of other advisers, and publication of newsletters. It prepares financial plans for less than 10 clients. On top of advisory services, the firm doesn't engage in other business activities.
