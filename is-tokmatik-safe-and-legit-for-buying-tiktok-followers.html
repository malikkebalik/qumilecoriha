Is TokMatik safe and legit for buying TikTok followers?

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Since the early days of social media, buying followers, friends, and viewers has been a common trend in those trying to become famous online. From Facebook pages forging likes, to YouTubers and Twitch streamers faking subscribers, people have been selling these fake fans to those willing to pay for fame. It’s no surprise there’s an app like that for TikTok as well: TokMatik.

Recommended Videos

Similar to its sister apps, TokMatik offers users the opportunity to pay for additional followers on their TikTok account, or likes and views on certain videos. With packages starting at $1.49 USD, it’s clear the site is aiming to get people of all budgets to buy into their service. The minimum amounts people can buy are 100 likes, 1000 views, or 100 followers.

If you’re looking to grow your TikTok following, it’s very tempting to buy into TokMatik’s offers. Getting thousands of views for just a few bucks seems too good to be true. But is it actually too good to be true, or is the site worth your money?

Is TokMatik safe to use?

Like most online retailers, the most important question is safety and confidentiality of information given to TokMatik. On their site, they make it clear that you don’t need to give them any login information for your TikTok to use their services. All they need is what account you want the followers for, or what videos you want the views/likes given to.

But is your payment information secure? According to their FAQ page, payment info is never stored on their servers. Plus, the tokenization process for sending your information to the credit/debit card providers is certified by Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards (PCI DDS). PCI DDS are security standards across all online retailers and are the basic protocols for keeping your payment info secure.

Is TokMatik legitimate?

While it’s clear your payment information is going to a real source, are the views, followers, and likes you’re paying for real? If you’re basing it off their 4 5-star reviews, then yes, it is. Claimed to be actual reviews from an email survey sent to customers, the reviews all tote how TokMatik gave them their TikTok views, followers, or likes in just a few hours for such a cheap price. Whether they’re using it to grow their TikTok engagement, or using it towards marketing on the app, the reviews all share stories of success with the service.

TokMatik’s website also continually totes their “authentic engagements”, guaranteeing you sustainable growth that won’t raise any red flags to TikTok. As they put it on their page, their likes, views, and follows “are real people”, meaning that engagement isn’t just a bunch of bots that will eventually get their accounts deactivated. The likes, views, and followers you get from TokMatik, they claim, will “stick around for the long term.”

So is it worth investing in the service? If you’re looking for a quick way to grow your following on TikTok, then TokMatik seems like the real deal. If the claims the site make are true, then you’re able to gain a significant amount of followers, likes, and views with very little effort. Plus, they offer first-time customers 100 free likes or views just to prove how effective their system is. If anything, might as well try it for free and see if the service is right for your TikTok needs.
