What is Morganna Roberts' Net Worth?

Morganna Roberts is an American entertainer who has a net worth of $500 thousand. Morganna Roberts was born in Louisville, Kentucky in July 1954. She is also known as "Morganna, the Kissing Bandit."

Roberts was known for rushing the field in baseball and other sports and kissing Major League Baseball players such as Pete Rose, Nolan Ryan, George Brett, Johnny Bench, Steve Garvey, and Cal Ripken, Jr.

Roberts has also been nicknamed "the grand dame of baseball" and "baseball's unofficial mascot".

via Getty Images

She also rushed the court at NBA games and kissed Kareem Abdul-Jabbar.

She has worked as an entertainer and dancer as well as an exotic dancer in Las Vegas, Nevada, Houston, Texas, and Oklahoma City, Oklahoma. Morganna has appeared on late night talk shows including Johnny Caron and David Letterman.

She posed in Playboy magazine in 1983, 1985, and 1989. Morganna starred in the films How I Became a Nudist in 1968 and Riverboat Mama and Indian Raid, Indian Made in 1969. She also appeared as herself in the 1996 movie Kingpin. In September 2014 she was the subject of the E:60 film MORGANNA: The Kissing Bandit on ESPN.
