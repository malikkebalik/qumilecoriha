Where Is Lizzos Watch Out for the Big Grrrls Cast Now?

July 2024 · 5 minute read

Fans are raving about how Lizzo’s Watch Out for the Big Grrrls cast is changing reality TV. Find out what Lizzo‘s Big Grrrls have been up to since wrapping the show.

Jayla Sullivan

Jayla wowed audiences with her impressive gymnastic skills during her audition for Lizzo. And she hasn’t slowed down since the show. Jayla recently shared a video of herself doing backflips in heels during a drag brunch performance. 

“Performing and dancing will always be home to me! Whether I’m at a drag brunch or on a stage the feeling is unexplainable,” said Jayla in her most recent Instagram post. “The experience on Lizzo’s Watch Out for the Big Grrrls reignited my passion and my love for dance. Every aspect of it had shaped me into a stronger version of myself I didn’t know was there.” 

Isabel Jones

Although Isabel was eliminated in episode 6, she hasn’t stopped bringing her K-pop influences to the dance floor. In a recent Instagram post, Isabel assured fans that she’s not going anywhere.

“We couldn’t be any more grateful to be surrounded by hardworking and inspirational individuals that truly care about us,” said Isabel. “This isn’t the end for us. Only the beginning!! Prepare to be sicka meee.”

Sydney Bell

Although Sydney made it to the final episode, she ultimately was not asked to join Lizzo’s tour. But that hasn’t stopped her from taking the dance scene by storm.

“Y’all see that little girl at the end of the video transitioning into the confident, young woman that I am today?” asked Sydney in a recent Instagram post. This is a testament to everyone that will be watching this show young and old, all ethnicities, and shapes and sizes that you are worthy and you are valid.”

Asia Banks

Asia lists herself as a professional dancer on her social media pages and has shared several dance videos since her time on the show.

Asia shared a photo of herself posing with Lizzo’s Watch Out for the Big Grrrls cast on Instagram. Along with the photo, Asia wrote “Sometimes I get emotional looking back over my life and all the trauma and hardships and can’t help but to be grateful for the gift God has blessed me with! Dance saved me! Through dance, I am healing, being able to build connections and grow with like-minded people…”

Moesha Perez

Moesha has continued to make appearances and teach classes since her time with Lizzo’s Watch Out for the Big Grrrls cast. She recently shared a photo of herself on Instagram posing at the Playground LA dance studio. Along with the photo, Moesha wrote, “I can’t believe I stepped foot into one of my DREAM studios!…I pray I teach a class here one day!”

Arianna Davis

According to her Instagram profile, Arianna is currently working as a dancer, actor, and singer. She’s represented by Take 3 Talent. Arianna shared a teaser video with Lizzo’s Watch Out for the Big Grrrls cast on Instagram. Along with the video, she wrote “y’all I can’t even put into words just how grateful I am to be able to be a part of this journey! It has been a rollercoaster ride from start to finish and this is only the beginning!”

Charity Holloway

Confidence was something Charity struggled with on the show. But that doesn’t seem to be a problem for her anymore. Not only is Charity a professional dancer, but she also founded an organization called 4Thirty-Two that promotes plus-size dancers and body-positive advocates. She’s also teaching classes at The Dance District in North Carolina.

Charity shared a photo of herself with Lizzo’s Watch Out for the Big Grrrls cast on Instagram. Along with the photo, she wrote, “This whole experience has been so epic and will forever mark one of the best times of my life.“

Jasmine Morrison

Although Jasmine was eliminated after admitting that she was the source of complaints from several other contestants, she hasn’t stopped dancing. In fact, Jasmine even responded to her elimination on social media. In the Instagram post, she assured both Lizzo and her fans that she’s “learned a lot” and told them to “prepare to see new me” before dancing to Lizzo’s “Truth Hurts.”

Ashley Williams

Ashley is currently working as a progressional dancer and entertainer. In addition to her dancing work, Ashley also hosts a YouTube channel where she shares videos of herself doing makeup, sharing hairstyling tips, and filming vlogs.

In a post on Instagram, Ashley shared a photo with Lizzo’s Watch Out for the Big Grrrls cast. Along with the photo, she wrote, “…let me just tell y’all This EXPERIENCE!!! Has been so life-changing for me.”

Kiara Mooring

Kiara lists herself as a professional dancer and choreographer on her social media pages. Based on her posts, it looks like she hasn’t stopped dancing since she left Lizzo’s stage. 

In an Instagram post of her dancing alongside Lizzo’s Watch Out for the Big Grrrls cast, Kiara wrote “Went from dancing in basement/hall parties to this song to performing to it on stage for one of the most iconic artists of our time…”
