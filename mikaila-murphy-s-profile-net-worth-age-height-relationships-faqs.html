TikTok

Mikaila has been active on TikTok since 2020. She started out making choreography and dance videos, but the most popular genre of dances on her account is “twerking”, one which has catapulted her to fame.

Since then, she has made music on TikTok, as well as posted clips of her doing voice acting and displaying her acting chops on her account.

Because of her TikTok content, she has been endorsed and has her content reposted by celebrities like Cardi B, Tyga, Neyo, Jonas Brothers, Bhad Bhabie, Jason Derulo, Lil Tjay, Mariah Carey, Derek Hough, Charlie Puth, Swae Lee, Meghan Trainor, Justina Valentine, Dream doll, Major Lazer, Tinashe and Kyle.

As of 2022, she has over 15.8 million followers on her account where she collaborates with celebrities and fellow creators to make TikToks. She is also a part of The Lab Content House, where she makes videos often.

YouTube

Mikaila has been active on YouTube since 2020, posting tutorials online, as well as cross-posting from her other social media accounts. She currently has 131k subscribers on YouTube. She also posts her music releases predominantly on YouTube.

Snapchat

Currently, Mikaila has over 250,000 followers on her Snapchat account, and has been a major platform for her since 2016.

Music Releases

Currently, Mikaila has been releasing music since 2021, and has racked up over 30k listeners on Spotify since then.

Dance Workshops and Career

Mikaila has conducted dance workshops in several parts and cities of the United States. She has had huge attendance of workshops in her home state of Michigan. She has taught all throughout the Detroit community including famous dance studios such as Exhibit 8, SKilSet Movement Studio, and collective studios, and also at educational institutes like INstitute of Dance based in Lansing.

She has also choreographed for high schools and different competition teams. She has taught internationally in Canada. Additionally, she has danced for, and worked with famous artists and creators like Iggy Azalea, Mariah Carey, Lele Pons, Danrue, Tonio Skits, Harry Jowsey, and Willy Wonka TikTok, Merrick Hanna, Lexy Panterra and Justin Bieber.

Acting Career

Mikaila has been featured in films like Gifted Hands, Flipped, Stone and The Irishman, as an extra.
