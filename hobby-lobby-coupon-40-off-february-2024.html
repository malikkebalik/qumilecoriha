FAQ

Does Hobby Lobby still have a 40% off Coupon?

No, Hobby Lobby announced in February 2021 that it was discontinuing its 40% off Coupon effective Feb. 28, 2021. However, printable Coupons can still be found on occasion, and there are tips from RetailMeNot experts on how you can still save 40% at Hobby Lobby.

Does Hobby Lobby use promo codes?

Yes, after you've finished shopping on Hobby Lobby's website, you can apply a promo code during checkout. Simply click on the red "Checkout" button to go to the cart page, and you'll see a box on the left side of the page under your list of items where you can enter your promo code. Type in the code and click "Apply" to see the discount reflected in your total.

Where do you put the Coupon code for Hobby Lobby?

The promo code box is located right below the items in your cart. During the checkout process, you can enter your Coupon code in this box to apply the discount to your purchase.

How often does Hobby Lobby have 50% off sales?

Hobby Lobby offers sales and discounts on various items throughout the year. Some specific categories, such as Craft Supplies and Fabric, have discounts of up to 50% off every three weeks. Additionally, Hobby Lobby provides 50% off deals on Custom Framing year-round with a Coupon.

Please note that the frequency and availability of sales may vary, so it's a good idea to check Hobby Lobby's website or sign up for their newsletter to stay informed about the latest sales and promotions.

About Hobby Lobby

Hobby Lobby began as a small, home-based frame making business in the 1970s and has since grown into a nationwide enterprise with over 900 stores. They offer a diverse range of products, including home decor, frames, craft supplies, paper crafts, art materials, and more. Whether you're a DIY beginner or a seasoned hobbyist, you'll find everything you need to unleash your creativity.

You can save up to 40% of your purchases when you are shopping with a Hobby Lobby Coupon.

Expect discounts as high as 70% on various items, including yarn, furniture, frames, craft supplies, and more.     Shipping Options at Hobby Lobby

    Hobby Lobby uses standard delivery to 48 contiguous states in the US. Processing orders takes 2-3 business days, and an additional 1-7 business days for delivery. Shipping fees are automatically applied during checkout based on the subtotal of the orders after discounts are applied.

    Hobby Lobby will use either FedEx, UPS, or USPS to deliver your items depending on the size and weight of the package during shipping time. Carrier surcharges may also be applied by the carriers, especially during peak seasons. Be sure to check out the table of shipping fees and surcharges via Hobby Lobby’s Customer Service information page. If you need expedited shipping, Hobby Lobby offers FedEx Priority Overnight Delivery and FedEx 2-Day Delivery for eligible items, but rules and conditions apply. You may contact this number, 1-800-888-0321 (Monday - Friday, 8:00 am to 5:00 pm Central time), before placing your order to confirm eligibility.
