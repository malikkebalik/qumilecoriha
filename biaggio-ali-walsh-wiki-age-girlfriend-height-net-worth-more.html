Meet Mohammad Ali’s Grandson, Biaggio Ali Walsh

Biaggio Ali Walsh (age: 24, September 4, 1998) is an MMA fighter whom most people recognize as Mohammad Ali’s grandson. This young athlete has been making waves in the MMA world for the past few years.

Walsh’s journey in combat sports took an unconventional path. Before stepping into the MMA world, he was a college football player at UNLV. Despite his success in football, Biaggio felt a strong calling towards the combat sports realm, just like his famous grandfather.

He made the decision to transition from football to MMA, and that decision has proven to be fruitful. With a unique background and an impressive skill set, Biaggio has become a rising star in the MMA community.

In June 2023, he was in the news due to his match against Travell Miller; luckily, he won the game due to his aggressive stance and added 1 more victory to his record.

Biaggio Ali Walsh Vs Travell Miller

On June 16, 2023, at PFL 5 in Atlanta, Biaggio Ali faced Travell Miller in a highly anticipated amateur bout. Demonstrating his skill and aggression, Ali Walsh overwhelmed Miller, earning a knockout victory in just 87 seconds.

This marked his fourth amateur win, all of which have been achieved through first-round knockouts. Now, the question arises of when Biaggio will make his professional debut as he continues to impact the MMA world significantly.

How is Biaggio Ali Walsh related to Muhammad Ali?

On September 4, 1998, Biaggio Ali Walsh (age 24) was born in Chicago, Illinois, America. His father is Bob Walsh, and his mother, Rasheda Ali, is the daughter of boxing legend Mohammad Ali.

He also has a younger brother, Nico Ali Walsh, a famous boxer like his grandfather. Growing up in Las Vegas, Nevada, Biaggio attended Bishop Gorman High School, where he showcased his athletic abilities, particularly in football.

After high school, he continued his football journey at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas, before eventually finding his passion in MMA.

A Real World Hero

Walsh once received a Certificate of Appreciation from LV Police. for saving a woman from an abduction. Accordign to sources, on November 13, 2022, at approximately 12:15 a.m., he was on duty at Omnia Nightclub when he observed that a male (adult) was trying to pull away an intoxicated woman away from her friend.

Without any hesitation, Ali intervened and made sure the woman was okay. Later it came out that the woman did not know the man who was trying to pull her away. The suspect was arrested, while the woman was released after a medical check.

That day Walsh prevented what could have been a violent situation. His vigilance and awareness were award-worthy, which is why Las Vegas Metropolitan Police Department awarded him with a Certificate of Appreciation.

Career in MMA

Despite having a famous surname, Biaggio Ali Walsh has faced the challenge of proving himself without relying on his family name.

He trains at the Xtreme Couture gym, working hard to hone his skills and develop his fighting style. Interestingly, while working as a nightclub security guard, few partygoers realize the legendary bloodline he carries.

Biaggio embraces his mother’s advice, emphasizing the importance of working harder to achieve success due to the weight of his family name. He remains humble and devoted to his practice, constantly seeking improvement in his career.

Walsh’s humbleness, devotion to his practice, and hunger to learn more is the reason behind his 4 victories (including the fight against Travell Miller).
