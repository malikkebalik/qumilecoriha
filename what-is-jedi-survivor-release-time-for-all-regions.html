What is Jedi Survivor Release Time for All Regions?

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Star Wars Jedi: Survivor sees the return of Cal Kestis (and, more significantly, BD-1). The official release date is April 28, but you may get a head start by downloading the game on April 25.

Below, you’ll find both the preload and full game release times.

Pre-load Release Times For Star Wars: The Last Jedi

Star Wars Jedi: Survivor will be available for pre-load on Tuesday, April 25 at 9 p.m. PDT for PlayStation 4 and Xbox One, and 8 a.m. PDT for PC. That means you may start downloading the game ahead of schedule on your PC right now.

That time, for those of us who live on the other side of the country and want to pre-load Star Wars Jedi: Survivor onto a console, would be:

  • 9 p.m. PDT on April 25 for the west coast of the United States
  • 12 a.m. EDT on April 26 for the east coast of the United States
  • 5 a.m. BST on April 26 for the United Kingdom
  • 6 a.m. CEST on April 26 most of mainland Europe
  • 9:30 a.m. IST on April 26 for India and Sri Lanka
  • 1 p.m. JST on April 26 for Japan
  • 2 p.m. AEST on April 26 for the east coast of Australia

Star Wars Jedi: Survivor is a massive download at around 155 GB, so make sure you have enough room on your hard disk before starting the process. Alternatively, you may go out and get a brand-new hard drive for your PS4 or Xbox One.

Survivor Launch Times In Star Wars: The Last Jedi

When the pre-load begins for your time zone on April 28, the whole game will be available. This is 12 a.m. Eastern Daylight Time (EDT). That indicates:

  • 9 p.m. PDT on April 27 for the west coast of the United States
  • 12 a.m. EDT on April 28 for the east coast of the United States
  • 5 a.m. BST on April 28 for the United Kingdom
  • 6 a.m. CEST on April 28 most of mainland Europe
  • 9:30 a.m. IST on April 28 for India and Sri Lanka
  • 1 p.m. JST on April 28 for Japan
  • 2 p.m. AEST on April 28 for the east coast of Australia
