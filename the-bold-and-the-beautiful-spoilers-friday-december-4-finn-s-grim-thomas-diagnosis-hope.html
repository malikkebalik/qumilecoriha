The Bold and the Beautiful Spoilers: Friday, December 4 Finns Grim Thomas Diagnosis Hope

September 2024 · 3 minute read

The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers for Friday, December 4, tease that Thomas Forrester (Matthew Atkinson) will be hurriedly assessed at the hospital, with John “Finn” Finnegan (Tanner Novlan) in charge and ready to make a big diagnosis.

Of course, thanks to Hope Spencer (Annika Noelle), Finn knows about Thomas’ manic and delusional behavior earlier in the apartment, and he suspects that Thomas might also have a traumatic brain injury that precipitated the behavior change.

On Friday’s episode of the show, Finn will have all the information he needs to render a diagnosis on Thomas – we’ll see what that ends up being, but since Thomas went in for a CT scan, it’s likely that the scan will show something pretty serious, like a tumor or some other type of brain injury.

Whatever the case, ongoing The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers for next week say that Thomas will need surgery to deal with the problem.

It looks like Steffy Forrester (Jacqueline MacInnes Wood) and Ridge Forrester (Thorsten Kaye) will rush to the hospital to be there for Thomas, so they’ll probably get updates from Finn on Thomas’ condition.

Of course, Steffy and Ridge will want to be with Thomas, who might still be unconscious from his collapse. We’ll see how things unfold.

Meanwhile, the tense scene over at Thomas’ apartment between Hope and Liam Spencer (Scott Clifton) will continue. As B&B viewers know, so far Hope hasn’t really been able to get a word in edgewise as Liam has been ranting at her about destroying their marriage and about what he believes happened last night.

Hope’s standing there in confusion, wondering what on earth Liam is talking about, since he’s the one who didn’t come home last night. Well, it looks like Liam will continue to rant and rave, making accusations against both Thomas and Hope.

We’ll see how long it takes for Hope to tell Liam that Thomas collapsed and that he’s off at the hospital in bad shape, and how long it takes for her to tell Liam that she never kissed Thomas and would never betray their marriage vows. That’s probably going to hit Liam pretty hard.

In fact, it may not be long before Hope tells Liam how Thomas was in a delusional state and acting as if the Hope mannequin was real.

That’s probably going to stop Liam dead in his tracks, as he realizes that what he witnessed in the apartment the night before wasn’t Hope and Thomas, but that it was the mannequin and Thomas. Liam will then realize that Hope never cheated on him, but that he cheated on her first.

Friday’s episode of B&B sounds terrific, so be sure to stay tuned! Stick with the CBS soap and remember that CDL is your hot source for all the latest Bold and the Beautiful spoilers, daily episode recaps, and news.
